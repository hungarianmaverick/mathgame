How to play?

Classic mode:

In Classic mode, your only goal is to solve as many operation as you can in 60 seconds! There are no consequences for giving wrong answers.

Survival mode:

In Survival mode your goal is to play as long as you can. You start with 30 seconds remaining, but every good answer gives you an extra 3 seconds. Time can go up to a maximum of 60 seconds. In this mode however, every wrong answer is punished with -1 seconds from your remaining time.
